How to Compress PDF Files

Our PDF compressor helps you reduce PDF file size while maintaining good quality. Here's how it works:

1. Upload your PDF

Drag and drop your PDF file into the box above or click to browse your files. We support PDFs up to 25MB in size.

2. Choose compression settings

Select your preferred compression level and adjust image quality if needed. For most documents, the medium setting provides the best balance.

3. Compress and download

Click the "Compress PDF" button and wait a few seconds. When complete, download your compressed PDF file.

Why compress PDF files?

Smaller PDF files are easier to email, upload to websites, and store. Our tool reduces file size by:

  • Optimizing embedded images
  • Removing unnecessary metadata
  • Compressing text and vector elements
  • Downsampling high-resolution images when needed

Is it safe to use?

Yes! All processing happens in your browser. Your files are never uploaded to our servers, ensuring complete privacy.
